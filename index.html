Tonga

Tonga is a Polynesian kingdom of more than 170 South Pacific islands, many uninhabited, most lined in white beaches and coral reefs and covered with tropical rainforest. The main island, Tongatapu, is home to the capital, Nuku’alofa, which has a royal palace and the Royal Tombs of the Tu’i Tonga dynasty. Tonga is known for its friendly people, rich culture, and beautiful landscapes.
